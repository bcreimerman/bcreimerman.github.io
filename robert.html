About Robert Creimerman

a rising Romanian pianist, he captivates audiences with his dynamic performances, award-winning talent and bold ventures into conducting and composition, crafting a mesmerizing journey through music.

A Collapsible:

Robert Creimerman, a rising Romanian pianist, captivates audiences with his dynamic performances and award-winning talent, honed at the prestigious Royal Conservatoire of Scotland. With accolades like the Scottish International Youth Piano Competition and collaborations with top orchestras, he showcases a repertoire spanning Beethoven to Liszt. Beyond performance, his venture into composition and conducting highlights his multifaceted artistry, making his story a compelling journey through classical music.

Compositions

Robert Creimerman writes music in a variety of genres for groups ranging from the smallest chamber ensembles to full symphony orchestras. Among his works he counts the full score to Isabelle Konrads "Purpur" (2022, 104 min), Boris Creimerman & Tilman Siegls "Crème Brûlée" (2025, 10 min) and a jazz trio album to be released in late 2025
